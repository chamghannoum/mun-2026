What is UOBDMUN

University of Birmingham Dubai Model United Nations (UOBDMUN) is where diplomacy meets dynamism! We're a buzzing community of students who love diving into global debates, solving world problems, and making friends along the way.

Whether you're a seasoned delegate or a first-time speaker, UOBDMUN is all about collaboration, creativity, and, yes, a little bit of competitive flair. From heated debates to exciting socials, we make sure every experience is as memorable as it is meaningful.
